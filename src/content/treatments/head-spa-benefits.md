## Why Seoul Travelers Love the Head Spa

If you're visiting Seoul and only do one beauty treatment — make it a **Head Spa**. It's one of Korea's most beloved wellness rituals and genuinely different from anything you'll find at home.

## What's Included

### Step 1 — Scalp Diagnosis
We examine your scalp condition: oily, dry, sensitive, or prone to hair loss. This determines which products are right for you.

### Step 2 — Deep Scalp Cleansing
A professional scrub removes weeks of product buildup, dead skin cells, and excess sebum.

### Step 3 — Treatment Application
Customized serums targeting your specific concerns — growth, hydration, or oil control.

### Step 4 — Aromatherapy Massage
A 20–30 minute head, neck, and shoulder massage using essential oils. Deeply relaxing.

### Step 5 — Hair Conditioning
A nourishing mask restores softness and shine — so you leave looking completely refreshed.

- Travelers with dry, itchy, or flaky scalps
- Those experiencing hair fall or thinning
- Anyone wanting deep relaxation after sightseeing
- Guests curious about authentic Korean wellness
