Myeongdong is Seoul's most famous tourist district — and hidden among the street food stalls, cosmetic shops and fashion boutiques are some of the best hair salons in Korea. Getting your hair done in Seoul is an experience unlike any other, and CHAHONG ROOM is designed specifically to make that experience accessible and enjoyable for international visitors.

## Why Get Your Hair Done in Myeongdong?

### 1. World-Class Technique at Accessible Prices
Korean hair stylists are trained to an exceptionally high standard. Korean salon techniques — from perms to color to cutting — are globally recognized as among the best. And compared to salons in the US, UK or Australia, prices are very reasonable.

### 2. Trendsetting Styles
Korea sets global hair trends. Wolf cuts, see-through bangs, C-curl perms, milk tea brown — these all started in Korean salons. Getting your hair done in Seoul means you're getting the trend at the source.

### 3. Hair Treatments You Can't Get Elsewhere
Korean head spas, scalp treatments and hair masks use formulations and techniques you simply can't find in most Western countries.

### 4. English Service at CHAHONG ROOM
The biggest barrier for international visitors is the language. At CHAHONG ROOM, our entire team communicates in English — so you'll never feel confused, misunderstood or unable to express what you want.

## Tips for International Visitors

**Do bring reference photos.** This is the single most important thing you can do. A photo eliminates language barriers and ensures your stylist understands exactly what you want.

**Be honest about your hair history.** Tell us about previous coloring, perms or chemical treatments. This helps us give you the safest and best result.

**Allow extra time.** Korean salons are thorough — a haircut includes a shampoo, cut, blow dry and styling. A perm can take 3–4 hours. Don't rush.

**Ask questions.** Our team loves explaining what we're doing and why. Ask about products, techniques, aftercare — we're happy to share our knowledge.

**Book in advance for complex services.** For perms, highlights and bleaching, booking ahead allows us to prepare and give you the best possible appointment.
