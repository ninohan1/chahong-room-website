## How Do I Know if My Hair is Damaged?

Signs your hair needs a repair treatment:

- **Excessive breakage** — hair snaps when brushing or styling
- **Extreme dryness** — hair feels rough, straw-like or coarse
- **Loss of elasticity** — hair doesn't stretch when wet, it just snaps
- **Frizz and flyaways** — even after conditioning, hair is unruly
- **Dull, flat appearance** — no shine, looks lifeless
- **Split ends throughout** — not just at the tips
- **Color that fades too fast** — porous, damaged hair can't hold color

If you recognize 3 or more of these signs, a professional repair treatment will make a significant difference.

## The Best Hair Repair Treatments at CHAHONG ROOM

### 1. Olaplex Treatment — Bond Rebuilding

**What it does:** Olaplex is a patented bond-building system that reconnects broken disulfide bonds inside the hair shaft. These bonds are broken by bleaching, coloring, heat styling and chemical treatments.

**Best for:** Bleached hair, over-processed hair, hair that breaks easily

**Results:** Stronger, more elastic hair with significantly less breakage. Many clients notice results after a single session.

**Price:** From ₩80,000
**Duration:** 60–90 minutes

---

### 2. Hair Botox — Deep Filling Treatment

**What it does:** Hair botox is a deep conditioning treatment that fills gaps and damage in the hair cortex with proteins, vitamins and amino acids. Unlike regular Botox, it contains no botulinum toxin — the name refers to the "filling" effect it has on hair fibers.

**Best for:** Frizzy hair, dry hair, hair that lacks shine, over-processed hair

**Results:** Dramatically smoother, shinier hair with reduced frizz. Results last 2–4 months.

**Price:** From ₩80,000
**Duration:** 90–120 minutes

---

### 3. Keratin Treatment — Smoothing & Frizz Control

**What it does:** Keratin treatment coats the hair shaft with a protein-rich formula that smooths the cuticle, eliminates frizz and reduces drying time by up to 50%.

**Best for:** Frizzy, unruly hair, those wanting smoother, more manageable hair

**Results:** Smooth, frizz-free hair for 3–5 months. Hair is easier to style and dries faster.

**Note:** Unlike hair botox, keratin treatments can slightly relax curl patterns. If you want to maintain your natural curl, hair botox may be a better choice.

**Price:** From ₩150,000
**Duration:** 2–3 hours

---

### 4. Deep Conditioning Hair Mask

**What it does:** An intensive conditioning treatment applied with heat to deeply penetrate the hair shaft. Restores moisture, softness and manageability.

**Best for:** Dry, dehydrated hair, hair that lacks softness

**Results:** Immediately softer, more manageable hair. Best as a regular maintenance treatment.

**Price:** From ₩30,000
**Duration:** 30–45 minutes

## After Your Treatment — How to Maintain Results

- **Use sulfate-free shampoo** — sulfates strip treatments faster
- **Apply a weekly hair mask** — keep the moisture locked in
- **Reduce heat styling** — let hair air dry when possible
- **Use heat protectant** — always before blow drying or styling
- **Sleep on a silk pillowcase** — reduces friction and breakage overnight
