## What Is a C-Curl Perm?

The **C-Curl Perm** creates soft, inward-curling waves at the ends of your hair — giving a naturally elegant, feminine look. Unlike traditional spiral perms, the C-curl is gentle and designed to look as if your hair naturally falls that way.

It's the single most-requested perm treatment at Korean hair salons, beloved by locals and international visitors alike.

## C-Curl vs. S-Curl — What's the Difference?

| | C-Curl | S-Curl |
|---|---|---|
| Shape | Inward curl at ends | Full wave from mid-length |
| Volume | Medium | High |
| Best for | Natural, elegant look | Glamorous, editorial look |

## Aftercare Tips

- Avoid washing for **48 hours** after the perm
- Use **sulfate-free shampoo** only
- Air dry when possible
- Apply **curl cream** to maintain the shape
- Touch-up every **4–6 months**
