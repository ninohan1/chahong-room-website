## What Is the Korean Wolf Cut?

The **Korean Wolf Cut** is one of the most iconic hair trends to emerge from Seoul in recent years. Blending the 70s shag cut with a modern Korean aesthetic, it features heavy layers, curtain bangs, and a textured, effortlessly cool finish that looks great on almost every hair type.

It became globally viral through K-drama and K-pop styling, and remains one of the most-requested cuts at CHAHONG ROOM.

## Who Is the Wolf Cut Best For?

- **Straight hair** — adds movement and texture
- **Wavy hair** — enhances natural wave pattern
- **Round faces** — layers add vertical dimension
- **Oval faces** — any wolf cut variation works perfectly
- **Square faces** — soft curtain bangs soften strong jawlines

## How to Style Your Wolf Cut

1. Apply a light styling cream or mousse to damp hair
2. Diffuse on low heat, scrunching the layers
3. For sleeker styling, blow-dry with a round brush
4. Finish with a small amount of hair oil for shine
