## Why Korean Scalp Care is Different

Korean scalp care goes far beyond a quick shampoo and condition. The Korean approach treats the scalp like skin — analyzing its condition, identifying problems and applying targeted treatments.

Key differences from Western salon scalp care:

- **Scalp analysis** — we examine your scalp with professional tools before any treatment
- **Targeted serums** — specific ampoules and serums are applied based on your scalp type
- **Multi-step process** — cleansing, treatment, massage and finishing are all separate steps
- **Head spa culture** — relaxation is built into every scalp treatment

## What Happens During a Korean Head Spa?

Here's what to expect step by step:

**Step 1: Scalp Analysis**
Your stylist will examine your scalp using a professional scalp analyzer. This identifies your scalp type (dry, oily, sensitive, normal) and any specific concerns.

**Step 2: Pre-Treatment Cleanse**
A specialized clarifying shampoo removes surface buildup before the treatment begins.

**Step 3: Treatment Application**
Based on your scalp analysis, targeted serums and treatments are applied to address your specific concerns.

**Step 4: Steam Treatment**
Steam is applied to open the pores and allow the treatment to penetrate deeply.

**Step 5: Scalp Massage**
A professional massage stimulates blood circulation, relaxes muscles and helps the treatment absorb fully.

**Step 6: Rinse & Finish**
Your hair is rinsed, conditioned and styled. You'll leave feeling completely refreshed.

## Scalp Care Tips Between Salon Visits

Our English-speaking stylists will give you personalized advice, but here are general tips:

- **Shampoo your scalp, not your hair** — focus cleansing on the roots where oil and buildup accumulate
- **Don't skip conditioner, but keep it off the scalp** — apply only to mid-lengths and ends
- **Rinse with cool water** — hot water can strip natural oils and irritate the scalp
- **Massage your scalp daily** — even 2–3 minutes improves circulation
- **Protect from UV** — the sun damages your scalp just like your skin
